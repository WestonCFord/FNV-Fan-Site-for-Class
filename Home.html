What is Fallout: New Vegas?

Fallout: New Vegas is an open world role-playing game set in the Mojave Desert after a nuclear war ravaged the world in 2077. It was developed by Obsidian Entertainment and published by Bethesda Softworks. The game is the fourth major installment in the Fallout series. Fallout: New Vegas implements the Gamebryo engine for its gameplay. It was released for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 3, and Xbox 360. It was released in North America on October 19, 2010, in Australia on October 21, 2010, and in Europe on October 22, 2010.
