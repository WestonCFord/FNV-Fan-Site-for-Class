DLC

Four story add-ons and two item packs were released as DLC for Fallout: New Vegas. The four story add-ons are Dead Money, Honest Hearts, Old World Blues, and Lonesome Road. The two item packs are Courier's Stash and Gun Runners' Arsenal. The normal max level in Fallout: New Vegas is 30, but each of the four story add-ons adds 5 levels to that, with a total of 50, if all of the add-ons are downloaded. The Ultimate Edition of Fallout: New Vegas comes with all of the DLC.


Story Add-Ons


Dead Money

Art for Dead Money


Dead Money is the first story add-on released for Fallout: New Vegas. It takes place in the Sierra Madre Casino, a resort that was both destroyed and preserved by the toxic cloud that engulfs it. It is inhabited by Ghost People, strange figures in gas masks that do not speak and will survive unless their limbs are cut off. Dead Money follows a scheme to rob the Sierra Madre's vault, set into play by the insane Father Elijah. Father Elijah has held the Courier, as well as others in the Sierra Madre. The only way to escape is to do Elijah's bidding. Dead Money is accessed through the Abandoned Brotherhood of Steel Bunker.


Honest Hearts

Art for Honest Hearts


Honest Hearts is the second story add-on released for Fallout: New Vegas. It takes place in Utah's Zion National Park, a location full of tribals. In Honest Hearts, the Courier becomes caught up in a tribal war, and a fight between a missionary and a burned man. Honest Hearts is accessed through the Northern Passage.


Old World Blues

Art for Old World Blues


Old World Blues is the third story add-on released for Fallout: New Vegas. It takes place in Big Mountain, stylized as Big MT, also called the Big Empty. Big MT was a place where pre-war scientists came together to make discoveries to benefit mankind. After the war, it's in total disrepair. In Old World Blues, the Courier must fight through Big MT to defeat the evil Dr. Mobius and get his brain back. Old World Blues is accessed through the Mojave Drive-In.


Lonesome Road

Art for Lonesome Road


Lonesome Road is the fourth story add-on released for Fallout: New Vegas. It takes place in The Divide, a canyon that is the epicenter of earthquakes and violent storms. In Lonesome Road, the Courier travels to The Divide, and gets in a conflict with Ulysses, the original Courier Six. Lonesome Road is accessed through the Canyon Wreckage.
