Main Characters


The main characters and their descriptions:

  • Courier Six: The player character. Not a voiced character.

  • Benny: The leader of The Chairmen, a minor faction in New Vegas. He is voiced by Matthew Perry, who is famous for playing Chandler Bing in Friends.

    • Benny, in the game's opening cutscene.

  • Mr. House: Robert Edwin House, the man in control of New Vegas. His security measures helped preserve New Vegas when the bombs fell.

    • An in-game portrait of Mr. House.


  • President Kimball: President Aaron Kimball is the current President of the NCR. He is either assassinated or rescued from assassination during the events of the game.

    • An in-game portrait of President Kimball.


  • General Oliver: General Lee Oliver is the current commander of the NCR's forces. The final boss of the game in one of the endings.

    • General Lee Oliver


  • Caesar: Originally named Edward Sallow, when he came to power, he took the name Caesar.

    • Caesar


  • Legate Lanius: Caesar's second Legate. The final boss of the game in three of the endings.

    • Legate Lanius


  • Yes Man: Yes Man is a Securitron that Benny reprogrammed to take over Mr. House's army and truly free New Vegas. He is voiced by Dave Foley, who also played Flik in A Bug's Life.

    • Yes Man
