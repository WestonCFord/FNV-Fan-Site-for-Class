Soundtrack

The cover art for the Fallout: New Vegas Soundtrack.


In addition to its ambient music, Fallout: New Vegas has various songs from the mid-20th century. The soundtrack has songs by Dean Martin, Frank Sinatra, The Ink Spots, and Kay Kyser. It helps the game emulate the feel of the late 1950s. There are two main radio stations in the game, Mojave Music Radio and Radio New Vegas.
